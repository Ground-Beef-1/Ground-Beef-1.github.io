For this recipie you will need:

Ingredient Amount
Potato (unpeeled) 0
Instant potato powder 1 liter
Water 1 cup
Rocks (from outside) A few

You will also need

  • A mallet
  • A large plastic bag
  1. Take the instant potato powder and mix with water in a bowl
  2. Put the now potatoed powder mix into plastic bag along with
    the rocks and the 0 potatoes
  3. Take the bag and put it on the countertop
  4. Use the mallet to mash the potatoes with enough force to break
    granite
  5. Now you have your bag of mashed potatoes(Indescernable slop)
