For this recipie you will need:

Ingredient Amount
Ripe Banana 3
Whole Grain Bread 1 loaf
Baking Powder 1/3cup

You will also need

  • A Bread Pan(81/2x 41/2)
  • A Working Blender
  • A Working Oven
  • A 1/3 cup measuring cup
  1. First, peel and slice the Bananas and put into blender
  2. Now you will slice the bread and put it with the Bananas
  3. Next you will turn on the blender to top speed for 5 minutes
  4. Dump solution into a bowl and mix in the baking powder until
    combined
  5. Put your dough into the bread pan and put it in the oven at
    350oF for 27 minutes
